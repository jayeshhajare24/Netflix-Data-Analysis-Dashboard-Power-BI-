# Netflix-Data-Analysis-Dashboard-Power-BI-
This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard built using the Netflix dataset. The objective of the project is to analyze content distribution, ratings, genres, countries, and release trends to extract meaningful business insights.
The dashboard transforms raw data into a clear and structured visual story that highlights patterns in Netflixâ€™s content library.

Objectives
Analyze total titles, ratings, genres, and production locations
Compare distribution of Movies and TV Shows
Identify most common content ratings
Determine top countries producing Netflix content
Analyze content growth trend by release year

Key Insights
Movies dominate the platform compared to TV Shows
TV-MA and TV-14 are the most frequent content ratings
The United States leads in total content production
Significant growth in content production after 2015
Dashboard FeaturesKPI cards for total titles, ratings, genres, and locations
Bar charts for genre and rating distribution
Donut chart for Movies vs TV Shows
Treemap for top 10 countries by content
Line chart showing release year trends
